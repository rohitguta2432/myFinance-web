FAQ

Common questions, honest answers

Is MyFinancial really free? chevron_right
Yes, the basic assessment is completely free. We monetize through optional premium features and expert consultations, never by selling your data. Our goal is to provide transparency first.
How is my data kept private? chevron_right
We use local storage processing where possible. Your sensitive financial data is processed in your browser and is not stored on our servers unless you explicitly create an account to save your progress.
Do I need to sign up? chevron_right
No signup is required to get your initial assessment. You can see your insights immediately. Signup is only needed if you want to save your report for later viewing.
Can I export my report? chevron_right
Yes, you can export your financial health report as a PDF for free. This allows you to discuss it with your partner or a financial advisor offline.
How accurate are the projections? chevron_right
Our projections are based on standard Indian market inflation rates and average returns on various asset classes. However, these are estimates to guide your planning, not guarantees.
What assumptions are used? chevron_right
We assume a standard inflation rate of 6% and conservative investment returns based on your risk profile. You can customize these assumptions in the advanced settings.
Is this regulated advice? chevron_right
No, MyFinancial provides educational insights and analysis tools. We are not a SEBI registered investment advisor. For personalized advice, please consult a certified professional.
